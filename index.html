reasons to Visit New York

Times Square

Central area in NYC that has many sops, restaurants, office buildings and flashing billboards around it

Central Park

Arguably one of the most famous parks in the world. Central Park is a manmade wonder. Not only is the first public park built in America, but it is the most visited, with over 25 million guests per year.

Empire State Building

Steel framed skyscrapper rising 102 stories that was completed in New York in 1931 and was the tallest building in the world until 1971. The Empire state building is located in Midtown Manhattan.

The metropolitan museum of art

The metropolitan Museum of Art, colloquially referred to as the met, is an encyclopedic art museum in New York City. It is the largest art museum in the Americas and the fourth largest in the world.
